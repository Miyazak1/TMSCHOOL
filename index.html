About TMPS Private High School

A focused online school for Ontario high school credits

International Students

Online OSSD pathways for students outside Ontario

International learners can use online OSSD courses to strengthen academic pathways, meet prerequisites, and prepare for future study in Canada or abroad.

Who it supports

Students seeking Ontario credits, prerequisite courses, English preparation, or flexible high school study.

How learning works

Online formats support different time zones while keeping teacher support and course expectations clear.

What to prepare

Current grade, prior report cards or transcript, target courses, and intended university or college pathway.

Study permit and immigration rules change. Students should verify requirements through official Government of Canada sources before making travel or visa decisions.
