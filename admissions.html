Admissions

From inquiry to online course access

Admissions is designed to be clear for families: we review your academic background, identify course options, and help you start with confidence.

How To Apply

A simple four-step process

  1. Submit inquiryTell us your current grade, completed courses, target program, and preferred timeline.
  2. Academic assessmentWe review prerequisites, transcript information, and your learning goals.
  3. Course planningWe recommend suitable OSSD credit courses and sequencing.
  4. Registration and onboardingConfirm the course plan, receive next steps, and begin online learning.

Helpful information to prepare

  • Current grade and school location
  • Recent report card or transcript
  • Target OSSD credits or prerequisites
  • University, college, or graduation goal
  • Preferred course start date
